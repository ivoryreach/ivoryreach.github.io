Section 01
Club Goals
Understanding what Cov Athletic are building and why content is central to getting there.
01
Competitive Excellence
Compete at the highest levels of small-sided (5-a-side and 7-a-side) football in the UK in order to be nationally recognised and professionally produced. The benchmark is Baller League.
02
National Recognition
Build a brand that is recognised beyond Coventry. Every piece of content is a step toward the profile required to attract sponsors and opportunities.
03
Youth Pathway
Develop a structured route for young players into the club. Content that showcases the quality and culture of Cov Athletic will attract the right talent.
04
Community & Charity
Active involvement in community work. Getting kids off the street, local homelessness support, food banks, and supporting international causes such as Palestine.
05
Sponsorship & Revenue
Create the opportunity for brand deals and sponsorship with current social media presence illustrating proof of concept but it needs a content strategy to scale it.
06
Documentary Legacy
A 5–10 minute club documentary and short player profile series (1–2 mins each) to tell the story of the squad, their backgrounds, and what the club means to them.

"This club isn't just about winning football games. It's about doing something meaningful for the community around us."

Execution
Caption Guide
How to write captions that perform and maintain the brand voice. Every post must credit Ivory Reach. This is a contractual requirement and a mutual growth tool.
Brand Voice
Tone of Voice
Confident. Direct. Community-first. Not corporate, not try-hard. Write like someone who knows the squad personally but takes the club seriously. Short sentences. Strong verbs. Let the content do most of the talking.
Structure
Caption Formula
Hook line (1 sentence — make them stop scrolling) → Content description (1–2 sentences) → Call to action (follow, share, tag a teammate) → Hashtags (5–10 max, relevant) → Ivory Reach credit (every post, no exceptions).
